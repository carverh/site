Cambrian Period

Enviorment

  • Warm and Humid
  • One large ocean
  • Glaciers melting and sea levels rising
  • Mountains just began to form
  • Lots of new sediment

Sight seeing

  • First formation of the Rocky Mountains
  • Pangea Supercontinent
  • Large glaciers
  • Lots of Trilobites, Sea Sponges, Brachiopods (Clams)

Things to bring

  • Breathable clothing
  • Tennis shoes
  • Water
  • Food
  • Something to kill time (Long trips across Pangea)
